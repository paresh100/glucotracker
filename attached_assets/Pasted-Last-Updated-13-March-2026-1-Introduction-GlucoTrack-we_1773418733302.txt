1. Introduction

GlucoTrack ("we," "our," or "the App") is a personal glucose tracking app designed to help users record blood glucose readings, medications, meals, and related preferences for personal record-keeping.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care.

This Privacy Policy explains what information GlucoTrack collects, how it is used, how it is stored, how it is protected, and what rights you may have regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

GlucoTrack collects only the information that you choose to enter into the app, including:

blood glucose readings, including values, units, timestamps, and optional context such as fasting or after meals

medication records, including names, dosages, frequencies, and dose log timestamps

meal logs, including descriptions, meal types, carbohydrate estimates, and timestamps

app preferences, including unit preferences, threshold values, display settings, and reminder times

GlucoTrack does not collect:

your name, email address, or phone number through the app

location data

device identifiers for advertising or tracking

behavioral analytics data

data from other apps on your device

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Your data may be stored:

locally on your device

in your personal iCloud storage, where iCloud syncing or backup is enabled

in exported files created by you when you use the app’s export feature

App preferences and saved records may use security features provided by your device and operating system.

If certain app features use network communication, that communication is protected using HTTPS/TLS encryption.

4. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide the app’s main features, including:

saving your readings, medications, meals, and settings

showing your history and trends

syncing or backing up data through iCloud, where enabled

allowing you to export your data when you choose to do so

protecting app access through supported device security features

5. Data Sharing

GlucoTrack does not sell, rent, or trade your data.

We do not share your health data with advertising networks, data brokers, social media platforms, or other users.

Your data may be processed through Apple services where needed to support iCloud storage, syncing, backup, or device-level security features.

6. Third-Party Services

GlucoTrack is not designed to use third-party advertising or tracking services.

The app does not use cross-app tracking for advertising purposes.

The app may rely on Apple-provided services and device features, including iCloud, local device storage, and supported biometric security options.

If other third-party services are added later to support app functions, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

7. Data Export and Deletion

You can manage your data within the app.

Where these features are available, you may:

export your data from the app

permanently delete your saved data from the app

remove locally stored app data by uninstalling the app from your device

Please note:

exported files are controlled by you after export

if your data has been synced to iCloud, copies may remain in iCloud until removed under your Apple account settings or backup system

uninstalling the app may remove local app data from your device, but may not automatically remove data stored in iCloud or in exported files

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Security measures may include:

device-based secure storage

optional biometric app lock

privacy protection when the app is moved to the background

automatic app lock after a period of inactivity

encrypted network communication where used

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but we work to protect the data handled by the app.

9. Children’s Privacy

GlucoTrack is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location and the laws that apply to you, you may have rights relating to your data, which may include:

the right to access your data

the right to delete your data

the right to export your data

the right to contact us with privacy questions
